about us

Welcome to Waterfront Pine, nestled in the picturesque valley of Laripora Pahalgam, where nature's beauty meets luxurious comfort. Our hotel is situated along the tranquil Sheeshnag river stream, offering guests an unparalleled view of the glistening water and majestic mountains adorned with pine trees. Spread over 48960 square feet of land, our boutique hotel boasts 10 beautifully designed rooms that are tastefully furnished to offer guests the perfect blend of comfort and elegance. Each room is equipped with modern amenities to ensure a hassle-free stay.

At Waterfront Pine, we believe that beauty extends beyond the confines of four walls, and we take pride in our 1.125-acre land filled with a private garden, perfect for guests to unwind and soak in the natural beauty surrounding the property. Our tagline, "Beauty Beyond Rooms," captures our essence perfectly, as we strive to provide guests with an unforgettable experience that extends beyond just their stay in our rooms. Our warm and hospitable staff is always on hand to cater to your every need, ensuring a memorable stay. We welcome you with a complimentary welcome drink, and our team is always ready to help you plan your stay and explore the local area. Located just 90km away from Srinagar, Waterfront Pine is the perfect getaway for those seeking a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Come and experience the natural beauty of Pahalgam, and let Waterfront Pine be your home away from home.

frequently asked questions

What amenities does the hotel offer?

The hotel offers a range of amenities, including a restaurant, free Wi-Fi, room service, Private Garden and Non-Smoking Rooms

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, the hotel offers free parking to its guests.

What adventure options are available for tourists in Pahalgam?

Tourists in Pahalgam can enjoy adventure activities such as white water rafting, trekking, skiing, snow-sledging, and angling.

What are some popular tourist attractions in Pahalgam?

Some popular tourist attractions in Pahalgam include the Betaab Valley, Chandanwari, Aru Valley, Mamleshwar Temple, and the Amarnath Yatra.

Are orthopedic beds available at your hotel?

Yes, our hotel offers orthopedic beds designed to provide proper spinal support and comfortable sleep.
